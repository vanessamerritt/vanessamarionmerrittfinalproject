How to Help

Help can come in many ways! Please contact us if you are able to lend a hand.

Foster

Foster parents provide safe, secure, loving shelter for the rescue dogs as they wait for a forever home. ABCR will make sure you receive a foster dog that is compatible with your home, family and experience.

Volunteer

We are always looking for dedicated people to help out in many ways. You can help in many ways, such as transporting animals and helping at various events.

Donate

All Breed Canine Rescue (ABCR) rescues dogs that other better-established mainstream groups will not help. Monetary donations, as well as food and pet supplies are always greatly appreciated.

Sponsors

Sponsors are businesses or organizations that have made a donation or contributed to our rescue efforts in some way.

About ABCR

All Breed Canine Rescue (ABCR), a small group of volunteers who saw the great need to rescue dogs that other better-established mainstream groups will not help. Some of our rescues are shelter dogs, or dogs that need new homes because their owners are sick, have moved, or their life situation has changed. Based in St. Thomas, Ontario. We cover Elgin, Middlesex and Oxford counties. We also work with rescue network across North America. We are a small new group of volunteers, trying to do a big job, with big hearts, few foster homes, and limited resources.
